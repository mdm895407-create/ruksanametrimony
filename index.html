Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Is registration free?

Yes, submitting your biodata is completely free. We only charge a nominal service fee after you select a profile and want to proceed with a meeting.

Do you share photos publicly?

Absolutely not. We follow strict Purdah policies. Photos are only shared with interested families after your explicit permission.

What happens after I send my biodata?

Once we receive your biodata, Ruksana Ji personally analyzes it. Within 24-48 hours, we will contact you with potential matches that suit your requirements.

Can I find profiles from other cities?

Yes, while our main base is Marathwada (Nanded, Aurangabad, etc.), we have a growing network of noble families across Maharashtra and Hyderabad.

How do you verify families?

We believe in quality over quantity. We personally call the families, often visit their homes, and check references to ensure the information provided is authentic.
